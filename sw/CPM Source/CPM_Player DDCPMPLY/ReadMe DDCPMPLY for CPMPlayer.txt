
DDCPMPLY is the GSX driver used in CP/M Player

Simulates a GSX screen in CPM Player emulator

features:
- B/W or 8 Colors
- Mouse support. Button 1 is left button, Button 2 is return key
- and FillArea
- 2 types of marker
- 1 font size, 1 line width
- no line patterns, no filled polygons, no text rotation
