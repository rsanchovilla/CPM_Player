Key Mapping on CPM Player

Joy 1 - controls 

	Cursor keys
	Joy button 1 - keypad 0 - clear screen
	Joy button 2 - keypad 1 - press to paint 
	Joy button 3 - keypad 2 - press to paint 
	Joy button 4 - keypad 3 - press to paint 

On program start, image is uncleared and filled with garbage. Press
Joy button 1 to clear it.

You can press at same time buttons 2..4 to draw in color combination
