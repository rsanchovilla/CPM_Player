
DEMO01 is a small program taken from the original definition of
Pascal, the PASCAL User Manual and Report by Jensen and Wirth.
Program 4.9, on page 30 of the second Springer-Verlag edition,
this program displays the graphic representation of
    f(x) = exp(-x) * sin(2*pi*x)  

To run:
A>PRUN PASCAL DEMO01
      .
      .
A>PRUN DEMO01
