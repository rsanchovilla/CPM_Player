ASTRDS.BAS

	This is my own version of the once popular ASTEROIDS
	video game we all knew and loved.  It uses a CUSTOM
	character set, but since only the first nine graphics
	characters are altered it shouldn't interfere with 
	other programs.  Full instructions can be obtained
	by running ASTRDS and pressing ESC.
